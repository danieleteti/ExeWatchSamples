What to look for in ExeWatch:
  • Logs page — Filter by tag "people" to see CRUD activity.
  • Timing page — "people.search" and "people.batch_import" entries show search and import performance.
  • Batch Import — Generates warnings for failed rows and an overall timing. ~20% of rows fail randomly.
